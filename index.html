About Me

Professional Summary

Senior Cloud Management & DevOps Engineer with 4+ years of experience across cloud infrastructure, automation, and container orchestration. Currently at Orange Jordan, where I manage the private cloud platform and a full internal DevOps toolchain.

My work spans the complete DevOps lifecycle — building and managing Kubernetes environments, designing CI/CD pipelines, deploying self-hosted tooling such as AWX, Vault, Harbor, Gitea, and NetBox, and maintaining large-scale private cloud platforms built on Red Hat OpenStack. I have a strong foundation in Linux administration, infrastructure-as-code, and cloud security.

I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Science from the University of Jordan (2020) and am RHCSA certified by Red Hat.
